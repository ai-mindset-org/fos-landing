## 2. Cost of Inference Collapse

### The price crash
- GPT-3.5-level inference: **$20 -> $0.07 per million tokens** in 18 months (Nov 2022 -- Oct 2024) = **280x cheaper** (Stanford AI Index, Epoch AI)
- GPT-4 equivalent: **$20/M tokens -> $0.40/M tokens** = **50x cheaper** in ~2 years
- Cost efficiency frontier dropped by **99.7%**: from $37.50/M tokens (GPT-4, March 2023) to $0.14/M tokens (GPT-5 Nano, Aug 2025) -- J.P. Morgan
- AI inference costs declining **10x annually** -- faster than PC compute or dotcom bandwidth

### DeepSeek disruption
- DeepSeek R1: **95% cheaper** than OpenAI o1 at comparable performance
- DeepSeek API is **27x cheaper** than OpenAI o1 -- costs only 3.6% of OpenAI o1 price
- A task costing **$50/M tokens on OpenAI** costs **$1-2/M on DeepSeek**

### Sam Altman's quote
> "It does in fact look like we're about to deliver on 'intelligence too cheap to meter.' We've been able to drive down the cost of each unit of intelligence by more than a factor of 10 each year for the last five years."
> -- Sam Altman, Federal Reserve conference, July 2025

### What $1 buys
- At current prices: **$1 = 14 million tokens = 10.77 million words** -- more than most people read in a decade

> Source: [Epoch AI LLM Price Trends](https://epoch.ai/data-insights/llm-inference-price-trends), [Fortune -- Altman "too cheap to meter"](https://fortune.com/2025/07/23/sam-altman-artificial-intelligence-too-cheap-jobs/), [DeepSeek R1 vs o1](https://www.rdworldonline.com/this-week-in-ai-research-a-0-55-m-token-model-rivals-openais-60-flagship/)

## 4. AI Consulting Market Disruption

### McKinsey in crisis
- McKinsey cut **~10% of non-client-facing staff** -- a few thousand roles over 18-24 months
- **200 tech roles** eliminated as AI automates back-office tasks
- Headcount dropped from **45,000 to 40,000** (5,000 reduction in 5 years)

- **51% of all web traffic** is now automated bots -- surpassed human traffic for the first time in a decade (2024 data, Imperva Bad Bot Report 2025)
- Malicious bots: **37% of all traffic** (up from 32% in 2023) -- 6th consecutive year of growth

### Industry impact
- Travel sector: **27% of all bot attacks** (most attacked industry)
- Retail: **59% of traffic** is bad bots

### Scale
- Imperva blocked **13 trillion bad bot requests** in 2024 across thousands of domains
- AI and LLMs are making bots harder to detect and easier to create
